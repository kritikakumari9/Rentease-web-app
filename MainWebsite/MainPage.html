About us
Rentease is the ultimate solution for hassle-free borrowing and lending within your campus community. Whether you are a student in need of a scientific calculator for a week-long project, a faculty member seeking a bicycle for commuting, a student in need of a drafter or a lab coat or anyone requiring various items for short-term use, Rentease has got you covered. Rentease aims to provide a platform to help students and college faculty members borrow items from property owners within the college easily. Through Rentease, a person can easily see a list of all items available for rent by and for the college members. It also displays other information, such as the availability and rating of items and their owner's contact details, which borrowers can refer to, which will make the renting process easy.
